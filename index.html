Our Founder

The vision behind Retray Real Estate Management

Yahya Maruf

CEO & Founder

Yahya Maruf founded Retray in 2005 at just 21 years old with a vision to transform the real estate management industry through transparency, innovation, and exceptional service. Now 26, Yahya brings fresh perspectives and tech-driven solutions to the real estate sector.

Under his leadership, Retray has grown from a small local operation to a widely recognized firm managing 759 real estate assets. Yahya's innovative approach and commitment to excellence continue to drive the company's success and reputation in the industry.

Yahya holds a degree in Business Administration and is a frequent speaker at real estate investment conferences nationwide. He is deeply committed to mentoring the next generation of real estate professionals and promoting ethical standards in the industry.
